Welcome to Galaxy Pets!!

Welcome to Galaxy Pets, a space-themed virtual pet universe inspired by classic online experiences like Neopets. Our project is a collaborative effort by a group of students who are passionate about web development and gaming. We are building a dynamic PHP and SQL website where users can engage in exciting games, earn points, and unlock a variety of accessories for their digital pets.
