New Cosmic Creature: The Stardust Jelly has arrived!

We are thrilled to announce the arrival of the Stardust Jelly, a new and adorable addition to our galaxy of pets! These charming creatures come in five vibrant colors: light purple, blue, green, dark purple, and pink. Discovered roaming freely along the asteroid belt, we've carefully rounded them up for you to adopt and cherish.

The Stardust Jelly is not only lovable but also eco-friendly, as they feed on cosmic dust, making them a perfect companion for any planet or ecosystem. Their unique diet helps keep their surroundings clean, ensuring a harmonious coexistence with other pets and environments.

We hope you'll form lasting bonds with these delightful creatures. Visit our adoption section to bring a Stardust Jelly home today and start your intergalactic friendship journey!
