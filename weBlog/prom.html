Blood ball

shamus took me out to columbia chicago's blood ball - he bought a suit with his mom at nordstroms the night before - and i got to pick out the tie colors with him while he was in the store - and he sent me one million pictures

blood ball was super cute and nice, and i remember the theme being enchanted garden - which reminded me of the homecoming i went to senior year in my high school - except for the homecoming i went by myself and i just took pictures with all my friends, because i didnt have a boyfriend i could take with me.

this dance was very fancy, because it was the first one where i got to take a boyfriend with me, and it felt really cute and special because shamus kept me in the loop of everything he was doing t get ready every step of the way. it is one of my most favorite dates of ours.

i also tried a new drink that night called yerba mate. i didnt like it.

shamus is my favorite boyfriend in the whole planet, which is why we are surrounded by space and sparkle to match how wonderful my shamus is

i hope to have one million more days with shamus that are just as magical and special and i already have. I also hope to take one million more photos for my new blog that i am making with shamus. i like websites.
