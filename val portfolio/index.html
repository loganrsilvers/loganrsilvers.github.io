About Me

Welcome to Val Powell's creative universe, where imagination takes form and everyday objects are transformed into extraordinary pieces of art. With a passion for crafting and a keen eye for detail, I specialize in a variety of mediums, from paper and fabric to wood and clay. Each piece in my portfolio is a testament to my dedication to craftsmanship and my love for experimentation. Whether it's intricate paper sculptures, whimsical fabric creations, or beautifully handcrafted wooden ornaments, my work reflects my belief that art is not just about what you see, but how it makes you feel. Join me on this journey of exploration and inspiration, where every creation tells a story and sparks the imagination.
