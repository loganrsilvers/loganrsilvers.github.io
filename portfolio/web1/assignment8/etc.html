Cult of the Lamb" is an action-adventure game developed by a studio called Massive Monster and published by Devolver Digital. It is noteworthy for its unique art style and gameplay mechanics.

Key features of "Cult of the Lamb" include:

  • Roguelike Adventure
  • Cult Management
  • Combat and Exploration
  • Dark Humor and Atmosphere

In the game, players assume the role of a cute lamb who establishes and leads their own cult. The management aspect involves recruiting followers, developing the cult's base, and making strategic decisions.

The game combines combat and exploration elements, where players navigate various environments, encounter different enemies, and engage in battles. The combat system is designed to be accessible but also challenging.
