Though this game is drawn in a cute art style, there are plenty of disturbing images and monsters. Dungeons are full of bones, burned villages, and runic symbols. One room of the map is full of characters who have been turned into golden statues. A dungeon houses mushrooms with humanoid forms inside, seemingly screaming. The antagonists, the Bishops of the Old Faith, are Lovecraft-like creatures with blood dripping down various parts of their bodies. The mini-bosses vary from bloated, oozing frogs to giant worms with rows of bloody teeth. When killed, one boss’ giant mouth folds open like a flower. The titular lamb levitates and bleeds from its eyes when engaged in a ritual, and the statues to gather devotion also host bleeding eyes.
